About Photography

Hello there ! Photography is the process of recording an image , a photograph , on lightsensitive film or, in the case of digital photography, via a digital electronic or magnetic memory. The photograph is evident in nearly every aspect of modern life.Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film, to create an image. The word “photography” comes from the Greek words “photos” meaning “light” and “graphos” meaning “drawing” or “writing.” It is a form of visual communication that has become an integral part of our daily lives. hotography is an art form that involves capturing light with a camera to create a visual representation of a moment or scene. Photography This art form has evolved significantly since its inception in the early 19th century and continues to evolve as technology advances.
